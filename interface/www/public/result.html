Measurement





At this time, CKM does not support outcomes of interest that are measured using correlation, rate, or survival. Please contact the tool's designers to request support for a specific type of outcome variable.




Effect Size
Without knowing sample size, we cannot calculate effect size.
Without knowing the count or proportion of participants in the intervention and control groups who experience the outcome of interest, we cannot calculate effect size for a dichotomous outcome.
Without knowing the mean difference between intervention and control groups, we cannot calculate effect size for a continuous outcome.
%
Without one of the above measures of reliability, we cannot calculate effect size for a continuous outcome.
At this time, CKM does not support statistical models that are not some form of linear or logistic regression. This follows from the limitation that CKM only handles continuous and dichotomous outcomes.
